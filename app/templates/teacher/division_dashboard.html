📐 Learning Dimensions Explained

SmartChat tracks four learning abilities. These help you understand what type of thinking each student excels at.

Logic — Problem Solving

🔍
Systems — Analytical Thinking
Earned through reasoning questions about cause and effect. High scores mean students understand how and why things work.
📘
Narrative — Conceptual Understanding
Earned when students explore new topics. High scores mean students actively seek conceptual knowledge and theory.
⏳
Spatial — Learning Consistency
Earned through daily quiz streaks. High scores mean students practice regularly without gaps in their learning habit.
