📐 Learning Dimensions Explained

A guide to help teachers interpret what each XP dimension means and how students earn them.

🧮
Logic — Problem Solving
Earned through calculation and formula-based quiz questions. High scores indicate strong numerical and mathematical reasoning.
🔍
Systems — Analytical Thinking
Earned through reasoning questions. High scores mean students understand cause and effect and can explain how things work.
📘
Narrative — Conceptual Understanding
Earned when exploring new topics. High scores mean students actively seek conceptual knowledge and understand theory well.
⏳
Spatial — Learning Consistency
Earned through daily quiz streaks. High scores indicate students who practice regularly and maintain strong learning habits.
